About Our To-Do List App

Welcome to our simple yet effective to-do list application! We've designed this tool to help you stay organized and productive.

  • Task Creation and Management: Easily add, edit, and delete tasks.
  • Task Prioritization: Set priorities for your tasks to focus on what matters most.
  • Task Completion Tracking: Mark tasks as complete to stay on top of your goals.

We hope this tool enhances your productivity and helps you achieve your goals.
